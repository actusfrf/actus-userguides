## Life Floor

**Acronym**: `RRLF`  
**Identifier**: `lifeFloor`  
**Group**: `Rate Reset`  
**Type**: `Real`  
**Default**: ``  

### Description
For variable rate basic CTs this represents a floor on the interest rate that applies during the entire lifetime of the contract.
For CAPFL CTs this represents the floor strike rate.
