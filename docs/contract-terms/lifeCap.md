## Life Cap

**Acronym**: `RRLC`  
**Identifier**: `lifeCap`  
**Group**: `Rate Reset`  
**Type**: `Real`  
**Default**: ``  

### Description
For variable rate basic CTs this represents a cap on the interest rate that applies during the entire lifetime of the contract.
For CAPFL CTs this represents the cap strike rate.
