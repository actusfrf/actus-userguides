## Initial Margin

**Acronym**: `MRIM`  
**Identifier**: `initialMargin`  
**Group**: `Margining`  
**Type**: `Real`  
**Default**: `0`  

### Description
Margin to cover losses which may be incurred as a result of market fluctuations. 
Upon contract closing or maturity, the MRIM is reimbursed.

### Allowed Values
- `Positive`
